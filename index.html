I have to admit, I kind of agree, as whatever I have put my mind to, I have achieved.

I tend to look at things in my life and wonder how to improve them. I identify my weakest points and I strengthen them. Hobbies

Yoga

I picked up yoga in September of last year and have loved it ever since. I think yoga is a very healthy hobby to have considering the health and physical benefits that it has on the body. Yoga makes me feel very dissolved, at peace, and present in the moment and my body feels activated after finishing sessions.

I usually do yoga before or after practice to improve recovery or prepare for activity. Doing yoga before workouts really improves how much you gain from the workout. Instead of being confined by your muscle soreness, you are able to commense the workout with complete readiness of what is to come. Rather than breaking down due to the difficulty of practice, you can rest and recover your body prior to going to sleep. I really believe yoga is a powerful tool that everyone can utilize.
